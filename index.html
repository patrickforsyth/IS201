Patrick Forsyth

Not the creative type, but a fun guy to be around.

Prove it!

First Impression

Patrick was raised in Parker, Colorado, where he played sports, made lipdubs, and randomly developed diabetes. He married the love of his life, Cassie, in April of 2019 after the two met in Frisco, Texas. The couple recently had their first child, baby Josephine, in October 2022. Their dog, Moose, would be offended to read this last sentence, as he has been around since they rescued him in January of 2020. But he cannot read, so please don't tell him. The Forsyth family lives in Orem, Utah, where Patrick works as a Business Analyst for Greenix while finishing his Economics degree at BYU.

Sense of Humor

One would be hard-pressed to spend any time around Patrick without laughing a time or two. However, none of these jokes are original. Patrick simply possesses a "trivia" mind (his words) and can recall obscure quotes from Seinfeld, New Girl, Brooklyn 99, and The Office with impressive speed. Though this quality may seem desirable, it often comes back to bite him, as many of life's situations are hardly sitcom appropriate.

Oh, the Potential

Not only does this "trivia" mind lend to Patrick's ability to feign humor, it also comes in handy while watching sporting events. In regards to Denver Sports since the early 2000s, Patrick is a walking encyclopedia. Imagine the good that could be done if this brain capacity were devoted to medicine or science! Much like Broncos fans who still remember the 2006 AFC Championship Game, we are left wondering, "What might have been?"

Pancreas Problems

Few conversation topics are universal, in that everyone has something to say. The weather. Politics. Opinions on Eggnog. But as of the Summer of 2014, Patrick has discovered a new topic to add to this list: diabetes. At this point, everyone either has it, or knows someone who has it. Well, Patrick happens to have it, and he sure does well at having it. Thanks to modern technology, Patrick's record A1C score is 6.1! If that seems completely foreign, ask around. It won't be long before finding a diabetic who can explain this.
